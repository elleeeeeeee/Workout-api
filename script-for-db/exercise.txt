Push-Ups

Description: A classic bodyweight exercise targeting the chest, shoulders, and triceps.
Performing Instructions: Start in a plank position, lower your body by bending your arms, then push back up to the starting position.
Target Muscles: Chest, shoulders, triceps.
Duration: 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions.
Rest Between Sets: 60 seconds.
Exercise Type: Strength Training.
Squats

Description: Compound lower body exercise targeting the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes.
Performing Instructions: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, squat down by bending your knees and hips, then return to standing.
Target Muscles: Quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes.
Duration: 3 sets of 12-15 repetitions.
Rest Between Sets: 60 seconds.
Exercise Type: Strength Training.
Plank

Description: Core-strengthening exercise engaging the abdominals, back, and shoulders.
Performing Instructions: Start in a push-up position with elbows bent and forearms resting on the ground, hold the position with a straight body.
Target Muscles: Core, shoulders, back.
Duration: Hold for 30-60 seconds.
Rest Between Sets: 30 seconds.
Exercise Type: Core Strengthening.
Lunges

Description: Lower body exercise targeting the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes, as well as improving balance.
Performing Instructions: Step forward with one leg and lower your body until both knees are bent at 90-degree angles, then return to the starting position.
Target Muscles: Quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes.
Duration: 3 sets of 10-12 repetitions per leg.
Rest Between Sets: 60 seconds.
Exercise Type: Strength Training.
Deadlifts

Description: Compound exercise targeting the posterior chain muscles including the hamstrings, glutes, and lower back.
Performing Instructions: Stand with feet hip-width apart, hinge at the hips and knees to lower the barbell towards the ground, then return to standing.
Target Muscles: Hamstrings, glutes, lower back.
Duration: 3 sets of 8-10 repetitions.
Rest Between Sets: 90 seconds.
Exercise Type: Strength Training.
Pull-Ups

Description: Upper body exercise targeting the back, biceps, and shoulders.
Performing Instructions: Hang from a pull-up bar with hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart, pull yourself up until your chin clears the bar, then lower back down.
Target Muscles: Back, biceps, shoulders.
Duration: As many repetitions as possible for 3 sets.
Rest Between Sets: 60-90 seconds.
Exercise Type: Strength Training.
Russian Twists

Description: Core exercise focusing on the obliques and improving rotational stability.
Performing Instructions: Sit on the floor with knees bent and feet lifted off the ground, lean back slightly and twist your torso from side to side, touching the ground with a weight or hands.
Target Muscles: Obliques, core.
Duration: 3 sets of 12-15 twists per side.
Rest Between Sets: 45 seconds.
Exercise Type: Core Strengthening.
Bench Press

Description: Upper body exercise targeting the chest, shoulders, and triceps.
Performing Instructions: Lie on a bench with a barbell above your chest, lower the barbell towards your chest, then push it back up to the starting position.
Target Muscles: Chest, shoulders, triceps.
Duration: 3 sets of 8-10 repetitions.
Rest Between Sets: 90 seconds.
Exercise Type: Strength Training.
Bicep Curls

Description: Isolation exercise targeting the biceps.
Performing Instructions: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, hold dumbbells at your sides with palms facing forward, curl the dumbbells up towards your shoulders, then lower them back down.
Target Muscles: Biceps.
Duration: 3 sets of 10-12 repetitions.
Rest Between Sets: 45 seconds.
Exercise Type: Strength Training.
Tricep Dips

Description: Bodyweight exercise targeting the triceps.
Performing Instructions: Grip the edge of a bench or parallel bars with hands shoulder-width apart, lower your body by bending your elbows, then push back up to the starting position.
Target Muscles: Triceps.
Duration: 3 sets of 12-15 repetitions.
Rest Between Sets: 60 seconds.
Exercise Type: Strength Training.
Mountain Climbers

Description: Dynamic core exercise with cardiovascular benefits.
Performing Instructions: Start in a plank position, alternate bringing your knees towards your chest in a running motion while keeping your core engaged.
Target Muscles: Core, shoulders, legs, cardiovascular system.
Duration: 1 minute.
Rest Between Sets: 30 seconds.
Exercise Type: Cardiovascular Exercise.
Flutter Kicks

Description: Core exercise targeting the lower abs and hip flexors.
Performing Instructions: Lie on your back with legs extended and hands under your glutes, lift your legs a few inches off the ground and alternately kick them up and down in a fluttering motion.
Target Muscles: Lower abs, hip flexors.
Duration: 3 sets of 20-30 seconds.
Rest Between Sets: 45 seconds.
Exercise Type: Core Strengthening.
Shoulder Press

Description: Upper body exercise targeting the shoulders and triceps.
Performing Instructions: Stand or sit with dumbbells at shoulder height, palms facing forward, press the dumbbells overhead until arms are fully extended, then lower them back down.
Target Muscles: Shoulders, triceps.
Duration: 3 sets of 8-10 repetitions.
Rest Between Sets: 60 seconds.
Exercise Type: Strength Training.
Russian Deadlift

Description: Variation of the deadlift focusing on hamstring and lower back activation.
Performing Instructions: Stand with feet hip-width apart, hold a kettlebell or dumbbells in front of your thighs, hinge at the hips and lower the weight towards the ground while keeping your back straight, then return to standing.
Target Muscles: Hamstrings, lower back.
Duration: 3 sets of 10-12 repetitions.
Rest Between Sets: 60 seconds.
Exercise Type: Strength Training.
Box Jumps

Description: Plyometric exercise targeting lower body power and explosiveness.
Performing Instructions: Jump onto a sturdy box or platform from a standing position, then step back down and repeat.
Target Muscles: Quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes.
Duration: 3 sets of 8-10 repetitions.
Rest Between Sets: 60 seconds.
Exercise Type: Plyometric Exercise.
Burpees

Description: Full-body exercise combining a squat, push-up, and jump.
Performing Instructions: Start in a standing position, squat down, kick legs back into a plank, do a push-up, return to squat, then jump explosively.
Target Muscles: Full body, cardiovascular system.
Duration: 3 sets of 10 repetitions.
Rest Between Sets: 60 seconds.
Exercise Type: Strength Training/Cardiovascular Exercise.
Renegade Rows

Description: Compound exercise targeting the back, shoulders, and core.
Performing Instructions: Start in a plank position with hands gripping dumbbells, row one dumbbell up towards your hip while stabilizing your body with the other arm, then alternate sides.
Target Muscles: Back, shoulders, core.
Duration: 3 sets of 10-12 repetitions per arm.
Rest Between Sets: 60 seconds.
Exercise Type: Strength Training.
Leg Raises

Description: Core exercise targeting the lower abs.
Performing Instructions: Lie on your back with legs extended, lift your legs towards the ceiling while keeping them straight, then slowly lower them back down without letting them touch the ground.
Target Muscles: Lower abs.
Duration: 3 sets of 12-15 repetitions.
Rest Between Sets: 45 seconds.
Exercise Type: Core Strengthening.
Jumping Jacks

Description: Full-body cardiovascular exercise.
Performing Instructions: Start standing with feet together, then jump while spreading arms and legs wide, return to starting position.
Target Muscles: Full body, cardiovascular system.
Duration: 3 sets of 1 minute.
Rest Between Sets: 30 seconds.
Exercise Type: Cardiovascular Exercise.
Dumbbell Flyes

Description: Isolation exercise targeting the chest muscles.
Performing Instructions: Lie on a bench with a dumbbell in each hand, palms facing inward, lower the dumbbells out to the sides in a wide arc until your chest feels stretched, then bring them back together over your chest.
Target Muscles: Chest.
Duration: 3 sets of 10-12 repetitions.
Rest Between Sets: 60 seconds.
Exercise Type: Strength Training.
